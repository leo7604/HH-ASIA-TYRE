<a name="booking-guide"></a>
## 📝 Step-by-Step Booking Guide

### Step 1: Choose Your Branch

**What you'll see:**
- Region selector (Manila or Laoag)
- List of available branches

**What to do:**
1. Select your region:
   - **Manila** → Shows 5 branches (Alabang, Bicutan, Bacoor, Sucat)
   - **Laoag** → Shows 1 branch (Laoag City)

2. Click on your preferred branch
3. Review branch details:
   - Address
   - Operating hours
   - Services offered

4. Click **"Next: Choose Services"**

💡 **Tip:** All branches are now open for booking!

---

### Step 2: Select Your Services

**Available Services:**

| Service | Description | Time |
|---------|-------------|------|
| **Tire Rotation** | Even tire wear for longer life | ~30 min |
| **Oil Change** | Full oil & filter replacement | ~20 min |
| **Brake Inspection** | Safety check for brake system | ~45 min |
| **Battery Replacement** | Test & replace old batteries | ~30 min |
| **Tire Replacement** | New tires installation | ~45 min |
| **General Maintenance** | Multi-point vehicle check | ~60 min |

**What to do:**
1. Click on services you need (select multiple)
2. If your service isn't listed, select "Other" and describe it
3. Click **"Next: Date & Time"**

⚠️ **Note:** Selecting multiple services may affect your appointment duration.

---

### Step 3: Pick Date & Time

**Available Options:**
- **Dates:** Today up to 6 months in advance
- **Times:** 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM (hourly slots)

**Understanding Time Slot Status:**

| Status | Color | Meaning |
|--------|-------|---------|
| 🟢 **Open** | Green | Available - Book now! |
| 🔴 **Full** | Red | Already booked - Choose another time |
| ⚫ **Past** | Gray | Time has passed - Cannot select |

**⚠️ IMPORTANT: One Booking Per Time Slot**
- Only 1 customer can book each time slot
- Once booked, the slot shows "Full" immediately
- This ensures personalized service with no waiting

**What to do:**
1. Click the calendar to select your date
   - Past dates are disabled
   - Weekends may have limited availability
2. Click an available time slot
3. See confirmation: `✓ Selected: 10:00 AM - 1 spots remaining`
4. Click **"Next: Your Info"**

💡 **Pro Tip:** Book early morning slots (8-9 AM) for fastest service!

---

### Step 4: Enter Your Information

**Required Information:**

#### Customer Details:
- **Full Name** - As it appears on your ID
- **Email Address** - For booking confirmation
- **Phone Number** - Philippine mobile (09XXXXXXXXX)

#### Vehicle Details:
- **Year** - e.g., 2024
- **Make** - e.g., Toyota, Honda, Ford
- **Model** - e.g., Vios, Civic, Ranger
- **Plate Number** - Your vehicle's registration

**Optional:**
- **Vehicle Trim** - e.g., GLX, EX, Limited
- **Special Requests** - Any specific concerns

**Example:**
```
Full Name: Juan Dela Cruz
Email: juan.delacruz@email.com
Phone: 0917 123 4567

Vehicle Year: 2024
Vehicle Make: Toyota
Vehicle Model: Fortuner
Plate Number: ABC 1234
Special Requests: Please check tire pressure
```

**What to do:**
1. Fill in all required fields (marked with *)
2. Double-check your email and phone
3. Click **"Review Booking"**

⚠️ **Validation Rules:**
- Email must be valid format (name@domain.com)
- Phone must be Philippine number (starts with 09 or +63)
- Plate number is required

---

### Step 5: Review & Submit

**What you'll see:**
Summary of your entire booking:
- Branch location
- Services selected
- Date & time
- Your information
- Vehicle details

**What to do:**
1. **Review everything carefully**
2. Check for typos in email/phone
3. Verify date and time are correct
4. Scroll down to see terms & conditions
5. Click **"Confirm Booking"**

**After Submission:**
- ✅ You'll see a success message
- ✅ Booking reference number displayed (e.g., `BK-20260425-001`)
- ✅ Redirected to confirmation page
- ✅ Confirmation sent to your email
- ✅ SMS notification (if enabled)

📧 **Save your booking reference!** You'll need it for:
- Checking booking status
- Rescheduling appointments
- Cancelling bookings

<a name="managing"></a>
## 📊 Managing Your Booking

### After Booking: What Happens Next?

```
1. You Submit Booking
   ↓
2. Status: "Pending" ⏳
   ↓
3. Branch Admin Reviews (within 24 hours)
   ↓
4. Status Changes To:
   ✅ "Approved" - Bay assigned, confirmed
   ❌ "Rejected" - Reason provided
   ↓
5. You Receive Notification (Email/SMS)
   ↓
6. If Approved: Show up at your scheduled time
```

### Booking Status Meanings:

| Status | Icon | What It Means |
|--------|------|---------------|
| **Pending** | ⏳ | Waiting for admin approval |
| **Approved** | ✅ | Confirmed, bay assigned |
| **In Progress** | 🔧 | Service being performed |
| **Completed** | ✓ | Service finished |
| **Rejected** | ❌ | Booking declined (see reason) |
| **Cancelled** | 🚫 | Booking cancelled by you or admin |

### Need to Change Your Booking?

**To Reschedule:**
1. Call the branch directly (numbers listed above)
2. Provide your booking reference number
3. Request new date/time
4. Wait for confirmation

**To Cancel:**
1. Call the branch or email support
2. Provide booking reference
3. Cancellation confirmed via email

⚠️ **Cancellation Policy:**
- Cancel at least 24 hours before appointment
- No-shows may affect future bookings
- Repeated no-shows may require prepayment

<a name="faq"></a>
## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Booking Questions

**Q: How far in advance can I book?**  
A: You can book from today up to 6 months in advance.

**Q: Can I book for someone else?**  
A: Yes! Just use their vehicle details and your contact information.

**Q: Why can't I select a time slot?**  
A: The slot is either:
- Already booked (shows "Full" in red)
- In the past (shows "Past" in gray)
- Outside operating hours

**Q: Can I book multiple services?**  
A: Yes! Select all services you need. The branch will estimate total time.

**Q: What if I need a service not listed?**  
A: Select "Other Services" and describe what you need in the special requests field.

**Q: How do I know my booking was successful?**  
A: You'll see:
- A confirmation page with booking reference
- Email confirmation (check spam folder)
- SMS notification (if enabled)

---

### Date & Time Questions

**Q: Can I book same-day appointments?**  
A: Yes! If there are available time slots remaining.

**Q: Why can't I book a time for today?**  
A: If current time is 10:00 AM, you cannot book 8:00 or 9:00 AM slots (they're in the past).

**Q: Are you open on weekends?**  
A: Most branches are open Monday-Saturday. Sunday hours vary by location.

**Q: What time zone do you use?**  
A: All times are in Philippine Time (PHT / UTC+8).

---

### Payment Questions

**Q: Do I need to pay when booking?**  
A: No payment is required to book. Pay at the branch after service.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**  
A: Cash, credit/debit cards, and GCash at most branches.

**Q: Can I get a quote before booking?**  
A: Call the branch directly for service pricing estimates.

---

### Technical Questions

**Q: The website isn't loading. What should I do?**  
A: Try these steps:
1. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Check your internet connection
5. Visit our backup URL if available

**Q: I didn't receive my confirmation email.**  
A: Check these:
1. Spam/Junk folder
2. Wait 5-10 minutes (email delays)
3. Verify you entered the correct email
4. Contact the branch to confirm booking

**Q: Can I use the app without internet?**  
A: Limited features work offline:
- ✅ View previously loaded pages
- ❌ Cannot create new bookings
- ❌ Cannot check real-time availability

---

### Branch Questions

**Q: Which branch should I choose?**  
A: Choose the closest location or the one with the earliest availability.

**Q: Do all branches offer the same services?**  
A: Most services are available at all branches, but some specialize in certain areas (e.g., Laoag offers suspension service).

**Q: Can I change my branch after booking?**  
A: No, you'll need to cancel and re-book at the desired branch.
