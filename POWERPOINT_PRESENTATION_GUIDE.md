## 🎨 **POWERPOINT DESIGN TIPS**

### **Color Scheme:**
- **Primary:** Yellow/Gold (#FFD700) - Your brand
- **Secondary:** Black (#000000)
- **Accent:** Blue (#1E40AF) - Supabase
- **Text:** Dark gray (#1F2937)
- **Background:** White or light gray

### **Fonts:**
- **Titles:** Arial Bold or Montserrat Bold
- **Body:** Arial or Open Sans
- **Code:** Consolas or Courier New

### **Layout:**
- Use consistent margins (1 inch)
- Align elements properly
- Use grids for multi-image slides
- Leave white space

### **Images:**
- Use high-resolution screenshots
- Crop to remove unnecessary elements
- Add borders or shadows for clarity
- Use consistent sizing

## 📝 **PRESENTATION SCRIPT**

### **Opening (30 seconds):**
"Good [morning/afternoon], we are [Team Name], and today we're presenting the HH Asia Tyre Digital Booking System. Our team of 4 members built a complete web application that modernizes how automotive service customers book appointments across 6 branch locations."

### **Problem (1 minute):**
"HH Asia Tyre was facing critical challenges: customers struggled with long booking forms, experienced double bookings, and had poor mobile experience. Staff had no efficient way to manage appointments manually. This resulted in lost revenue, frustrated customers, and inefficient operations."

### **Solution (1 minute):**
"Our solution is a comprehensive booking and management system with three main components: a customer-facing booking portal with a 6-step guided flow, branch admin dashboards for staff to manage appointments, and a super admin system to oversee all operations. We built this using React, Supabase, and deployed it on Vercel, with AI assistance from Qwen throughout development."

### **Features (2 minutes):**
"Let me highlight our key features. First, our Smart Booking System prevents double bookings with real-time availability and only allows one customer per time slot. Second, our Multi-Branch Management supports all 6 branches with independent admin dashboards and bay assignment. Third, our Role-Based Security ensures data protection with three distinct user roles and Row Level Security policies."

### **Demo (3 minutes):**
"Now I'll show you the system in action. [Follow demo script from Slide 9-10]"

### **Conclusion (30 seconds):**
"In conclusion, we've successfully built a production-ready booking system that solves real business problems. The system is secure, scalable, and user-friendly. Thank you, and we're happy to answer any questions!"
