## ⚡ HOW TO BOOK (3 Minutes)

### Step 1: Visit Website
🌐 **hh-asia-tyre.vercel.app**

### Step 2: Click "Book Now"
Top navigation or homepage button

### Step 3: Fill in Details
1️⃣ **Select Branch** - Choose nearest location  
2️⃣ **Choose Services** - Pick what you need  
3️⃣ **Pick Date & Time** - Available slots shown in green  
4️⃣ **Enter Your Info** - Name, phone, vehicle details  

### Step 4: Submit & Save Reference
✅ Booking reference: `BK-YYYYMMDD-XXX`  
✅ Confirmation sent to email  
✅ Save reference for tracking
